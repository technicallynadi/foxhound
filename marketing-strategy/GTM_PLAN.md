# Foxhound Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 1. Executive Summary
Foxhound is the first high-precision AI Career Agent designed to handle the end-to-end job search lifecycle. Unlike generic "auto-apply" bots that prioritize volume, Foxhound prioritizes **callback rates** through deep matching, company research, and curated outreach.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Primary:** Senior Tech Professionals (Engineers, Designers, PMs) targeting roles at top-tier companies (Anthropic, Vercel, Figma, etc.).
- **Secondary (Verticals):** 
  - AI Developer Tooling (Engineers/DevRel)
  - AI Agent Security (Security Engineers)
  - Property Management (Managers/Operations)
  - Restaurant Operations (Managers/Owners)
  - Recruiting (Talent Acquisition professionals)

## 3. Value Proposition
"Stop Applying. Start Waking Up to Progress."
- **Autonomy:** Works 24/7 in the background.
- **Precision:** 70%+ match floor ensures high callback rates.
- **Intelligence:** Live web research on companies and people.
- **Control:** Multichannel steering (Slack, SMS, Discord).

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Primary:** Callback Rate (Interviews / Applications Submitted).
- **Secondary:** 
  - User Activation (Resume uploaded + First match reviewed).
  - Retention (Weekly active steering).
  - Referral Rate.

## 5. Execution Roadmap

### Phase 1: Early Access & Refinement (Current)
- **Focus:** Gather data on callback rates for high-match roles.
- **Channels:** Word of mouth, specialized Slack/Discord communities (LocalLLaMA, Cybersecurity).
- **Lead Magnet:** Free "Foxhound Brief" for a user's top target company.

### Phase 2: Vertical Launch (Q2 2026)
- **Focus:** Deploy industry-specific landing pages and messaging.
- **Priority Verticals:**
  - **Property Management:** Target Operations and Property Managers in `r/propertymanagement`. Partner with industry niche job boards for "Ghost Job Verification".
  - **AI Developer Tooling:** Target Senior Engineers in `r/LocalLLaMA` and Discord. Highlight MCP and LLM-specific matching precision.
- **Lead Magnet:** Free "Foxhound Brief" + "Ghost Risk Audit" for a user's top target company.
- **New KPI:** Ghost Job Avoidance Rate (% of matches flagged as "Ghost" before user applies).

### Phase 3: Platform Expansion (Q3 2026)
- **Focus:** B2B pilot for small-to-midsize recruiting firms.
- **Feature:** "Candidate Dossier" - Foxhound-generated research that recruiters can use to prep candidates.

## 6. Budget Allocation
- **40% Content & SEO:** Technical blogs on AI career agents, callback optimization.
- **30% Community Management:** Direct engagement in target Reddit/Discord channels.
- **20% Performance Marketing:** Highly targeted LinkedIn ads for "AI Security" and "Property Management" roles.
- **10% Experiments:** Referral incentives and "Lead Magnet" distribution.
