# Foxhound Verticals Expansion Strategy

## 1. Vertical Strategy Overview
Foxhound leverages domain-specific intelligence (terms, tools, communities) to deliver industry-specific "precision" that generic tools cannot match.

## 2. Priority Verticals (Q2 2026)

### Vertical A: AI Developer Tooling & Agents
- **Market:** Senior Engineers and DevRel professionals.
- **Messaging:** "Foxhound understands your stack." Mention Cursor, Copilot, LangChain, and MCP in matching.
- **Growth Channel:** LocalLLaMA Reddit, specialized Discord channels.
- **Proof Point:** Foxhound identifies the "Ghost Job" risk in tech postings by checking live repo activity/GitHub presence.

### Vertical B: Property Management
- **Market:** Operations and Property Managers.
- **Messaging:** "Automate your operations career." Focus on tool proficiency (Appfolio, Yardi, Buildium).
- **Growth Channel:** propertymanagement Reddit, Capterra/G2 review cross-targeting.
- **Proof Point:** "Domain-specific matching" – Foxhound knows the difference between a "Leasing Agent" and a "Leasing Manager" in a way generic LLMs don't.

## 3. Exploratory Verticals (Q3 2026)

### Vertical C: Restaurant Operations
- **Market:** GMs and Owners of high-end hospitality.
- **Messaging:** "Precision for the FOH/BOH transition."
- **Growth Channel:** restaurateur Reddit, POS tool communities (Toast/Square).

### Vertical D: AI Agent Security
- **Market:** Cybersecurity professionals focused on agent reliability.
- **Messaging:** "The secure choice for your next move."
- **Growth Channel:** cybersecurity Reddit, OWASP communities.
